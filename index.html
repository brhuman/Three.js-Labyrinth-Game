Game Description

You have awakened in an endlessly shifting maze. Every level is generated procedurally. Find the exit portal to progress deeper into the labyrinth.

Survival Guide

1. Find the Exit: Each maze has one exit. It glows with a faint light. Find it to survive.

2. Avoid the Stalker: A creature lives here. If you hear heavy breathing or footsteps, it's close.

3. Conserve Light: Your flashlight battery drains. Use it only when necessary. Find 🔋 to recharge.

4. Crouch to Survive: Some areas have low beams. Press 'C' or 'Ctrl' to crouch and pass under them.

5. Use Orbs: 🔵 increases your speed. 🟡 slows down the monster.
